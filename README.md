# Fitness-App
Apex Fit is a modern Flutter-based fitness and health tracking app designed to help users achieve their goals through smart calorie management, personalized workout planning, meal suggestions, habit tracking, and AI-powered health insights.

# 🏆 Apex Fit

> Train smarter. Eat better. Become your peak.

Apex Fit is a professional Flutter-based fitness, diet, and health tracking application that helps users achieve their fitness goals through personalized calorie tracking, meal planning, workout routines, and progress analytics.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Health Tracking
- BMI Calculator
- BMR & TDEE Calculator
- Personalized calorie targets
- Weight loss, weight gain, fat loss, and maintenance goals

### 🍽 Nutrition
- Daily calorie manager
- Meal logging system
- High-calorie meal suggestions
- Low-calorie meal suggestions
- Fixed calorie meal options (500 kcal, etc.)
- Protein intake tracking

### 💪 Workout Planner
- Gym mode and cardio mode
- User-defined workout days
- Male and female workout plans
- Bulking and cutting plans
- Exercise sets and reps
- Push/Pull/Legs support

### 📅 Habit Tracking
- Water intake
- Sleep tracking
- Workout completion
- Habit completion status
- Daily adherence score

### 📈 Reports & Analytics
- Daily report
- Weekly report
- Monthly report
- Progress charts
- Suggestions and insights

### 🤖 Smart Recommendations
- Meal recommendations
- Workout suggestions
- Health insights

### 🔔 Notifications
- Workout reminders
- Water reminders
- Meal reminders
